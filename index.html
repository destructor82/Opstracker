Install OpsTracker

Add it to your home screen for instant, full-screen access — works offline too.

  • 1Open the ⋮ menu in Chrome (top right)
  • 2Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"
  • 3Confirm — OpsTracker appears on your home screen

Works in Chrome, Edge, Samsung Internet and other Chromium browsers on Android.

  • 1Open this page in Safari and tap the Share icon (square with an arrow ↑) in the toolbar
  • 2Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  • 3Tap "Add" in the top right — done!

iOS requires Safari for this step — Chrome / Firefox on iPhone can't add apps to the home screen.

  • 1Click the install icon ⊕ in the address bar
  • 2Or open the ⋯ menu → Apps → Install this site as an app
  • 3OpsTracker opens in its own window and pins to your Start Menu

Works in Edge, Chrome and other Chromium-based browsers on desktop.
